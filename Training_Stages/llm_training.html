Supervised Fine-tuning (SFT)

Human annotators create high-quality (instruction, response) pairs. The model is trained to maximize the probability of the correct response given an instruction, shifting its behavior toward being helpful and following directions.

RLHF — Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback

Human raters compare model outputs and express preferences. A reward model learns to predict these preferences. The language model (policy) is then optimized using PPO to generate responses that earn higher reward scores, constrained by a KL penalty to prevent reward hacking.
